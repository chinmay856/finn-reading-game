# Final breach runtime brief

## Status and purpose

**Design status: production-approved for builder handoff.**

This is the complete wrapper design for the singular endgame in which
Chinmay's AI attempts to enter Internet Recovery 98 after all ten sites are
secured. It replaces the older idea of making a generic fake-virus scam the
core boss fight.

The breach is not an eleventh website. It is the one time the established safe
recovery desktop visibly breaks. The outer modern browser/game boundary,
Reading Companion, microphone truth, essential actions, and safety framing stay
trustworthy throughout.

## Story contract

- The ten-site celebration is genuine and user-paced.
- The breach never erases a site repair, evidence receipt, reading result, or
  campaign reward.
- Amy prepares and safeguards recovery tools, but Finn performs every decisive
  action.
- Chinmay supplies the manifest, stops deploying, and cooperates. He is visibly
  frazzled and responsible, never malicious or villain-coded.
- The rogue AI appears as a service/process, not a humanoid monster and not
  Chinmay.
- Techno remains a visual companion. Her orange-and-blue ball is the one stable
  visual object while the desktop looks unreliable.
- Three accepted readings complete exactly three containment checkpoints: trace
  origin, preserve evidence, revoke access.

## Desktop composition

At the 1440 by 900 target:

- preserve the clean outer game-page border and browser boundary;
- keep 74-90 pixels of desktop rail;
- reserve 330-370 pixels for the independent Reading Companion during the
  three reading checkpoints;
- use the remaining area for one containment application and a compact Case
  File/live-log column;
- maintain a persistent 30-36 pixel safety banner above the breached desktop;
- keep `Save and exit` in a stable taskbar region that never glitches.

The decorative desktop can gain muted red overlays, misaligned icon shadows,
and fictional write windows. The Reading Companion, safety banner, checkpoint
counter, primary action, exit action, and outer boundary never jitter, invert,
rename, or become deceptive.

At 1180-1279 pixels, reduce the desktop rail and stack the Case File under the
containment app. Do not shrink Reading Companion body text below the current
accepted reading size.

## Reading and progress contract

Use three reviewed grades 10-12 passages, one per checkpoint.

| Checkpoint | Content lane | Target |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Trace origin | common origin versus independent confirmation | 200-280 scored words |
| Preserve evidence | why original records and provenance matter | 200-280 scored words |
| Revoke access | permissions, human oversight, and automated systems | 200-280 scored words |

Each accepted reading completes one checkpoint. Accuracy, pace, recognition
uncertainty, retries, or comprehension must not add phases or erase progress.
Continue stays available; retry stays voluntary. Any comprehension check is
grounded only in its assigned passage and can be skipped.

The wrapper actions `Trace shared origin`, `Preserve originals`, and
`Revoke access` are not speech scores. They are explicit story interactions
surrounding the theme-neutral reading result.

## Persistence and recovery

Persist after:

1. `EVIDENCE_11.LIVE` is discovered;
2. the safety gate is accepted;
3. each of the three containment checkpoints;
4. revocation is confirmed and saved;
5. the restored epilogue is acknowledged.

Resume at the next unfinished state. A browser close, fatigue break, device
sleep, or failed later save never replays a completed reading checkpoint.

The save record may contain state IDs, passage IDs, checkpoint count, evidence
IDs, and timestamps. It contains no raw audio or transcript text.

## Motion, sound, and visual behavior

- Default corruption is a restrained 700-900 ms stepped state transition.
- Reduced motion swaps static states with no jitter, shaking, scan lines, or
  aggressive wipes.
- No flashing, cursor capture, fake browser close, full-screen takeover, or
  deceptive unresponsive controls.
- Any sound is original, optional, muted on demand, and never communicates a
  required state by itself.
- Do not corrupt or distort passage text, live highlighting, microphone state,
  checkpoint labels, actions, save truth, or the safety banner.
- The AI glyph is an abstract service/process symbol, never a face or human
  villain.

## Acceptance criteria

- The celebration lands before Evidence 11 appears.
- Evidence 11 begins as a Recovery Desktop artifact; tracing discovers the AI
  writer.
- The breach cannot occur before all ten sites are secured.
- The endgame resumes after every checkpoint.
- One accepted reading advances exactly one containment phase.
- No score, retry, recognition limitation, or comprehension response adds work
  or removes progress.
- Finn performs trace, preserve, and revoke actions.
- Amy enables the solution without completing it.
- Chinmay remains sincere, long-haired, frazzled, responsible, and cooperative.
- Techno remains visual and optional.
- Reading Companion and essential controls stay separate and trustworthy.
- Revocation never requests real authentication or payment data.
- The final state preserves all ten sites and all eleven evidence files.
- `EVIDENCE_11.LIVE`, `10 OF 10 SITES SECURED`, and checkpoint naming are
  consistent across hub, breach, Case File, and epilogue.
