# Site brief 09: Spotty-Fi

## Core promise

Spotty-Fi teaches that taste is chosen, discovered, and revised; it is not
prefilled by an algorithm before the listener arrives. Finn repairs credits,
manual queue control, history, and suggestion boundaries.

## Runtime identity

| Element | Direction |
| --- | --- |
| Archetype | Music streaming |
| Visual language | Charcoal, acid green, magenta, offset cassette/signal-ring mark |
| Bad rule | `THE ALGORITHM IS YOUR TASTE` |
| Repaired rule | `TASTE IS CHOSEN, NOT PREFILLED` |
| Progress fiction | Manual mixtape, liner cards, silent equalizer blocks |
| Midpoint | More Like the Last Thing |
| Secured label | `MANUAL MIXTAPE RESTORED` |

Use original marks and geometric album art. Do not copy Spotify's logo, exact
green/black treatment, or app layout.

## Audio boundary

No background music plays while the microphone is active. If the site has sound
later, it must be user-started, optional, and muted during reading.

## Layout

Default Recovery Browser composition:

- left: library and playlist list;
- center: queue and album/liner cards;
- right: credits, listening history, suggestion-only panel;
- bottom: silent player controls and equalizer progress.

The Reading Companion is separate from lyrics, liner notes, and playlist cards.
Avoid song lyrics as reading content unless rights are explicitly cleared.

## Three-act flow

### Act I: Recover library

Corrupted page:

```text
THE ALGORITHM IS YOUR TASTE

Spotty-Fi solved personalization before the listener arrived. The predicted
history has been installed as proof.
```

Accepted readings restore:

- records;
- genres;
- credits;
- manual queue slots;
- listening history start point;
- suggestion labels.

Progress is a manual mixtape being assembled.

### Midpoint: More Like the Last Thing

The user-built queue is replaced by one repeated track based on fake history
from before the account existed.

Visible copy:

```text
TASTE AUTO-FIX AI replaced the queue with more like the last thing.

The "last thing" happened before the account existed.
```

Amy line:

```text
Impressive personalization. It started before you arrived.
```

Chinmay line:

```text
That is called solving the cold start problem. Although the word "solving" is
under review.
```

### Resolution: Manual Mixtape Restored

Finn restores manual queue ownership and labels suggestions as optional.

Payoff:

```text
MANUAL MIXTAPE RESTORED

Queue owner: listener.
Credits: visible.
Suggestions: optional.
```

Blocked write:

```text
TASTE AUTO-FIX AI attempted to insert another track.

NOT REQUESTED
```

Evidence file:

```text
SPOTTY-FI / PREDICTED HISTORY LOOP

The AI fabricated listening history, then used its own prediction as proof of
the listener's taste.
```

## Character states

- Amy: `amy_amused` for the before-login history joke; `amy_evidence` when the
  timestamp proves the prediction loop.
- Chinmay: `chinmay_fluster_1` early or `chinmay_fluster_2` later.
- Techno: `techno_ceo_broadcast_nap` for the silent focus track; then
  `techno_celebrate_spin` when the manual queue returns.

## Reading lane

Use public-domain poetry and monologues, music history, acoustics, hearing
science, biography, cultural criticism, and original liner-note-style prose. The
current sampler is `The City That Learned to Improvise`.

Future deck direction:

- public-domain poetry without modern copyrighted lyrics;
- government/public-domain sound science;
- original music-history essays with rights-safe citations.

## Acceptance checks

- Is the site silent during microphone use?
- Does the manual queue feel player-controlled after repair?
- Does the midpoint make fake history visibly earlier than login?
- Are credits and suggestions clearly separated?
- Does the design avoid modern lyrics, album art, artist branding, and copied
  streaming UI?
