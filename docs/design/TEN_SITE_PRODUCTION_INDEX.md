# Ten-site production design index

## What `production design complete` means

For this handoff, production design complete means the builder does not need to
invent a site's lesson, visual identity, layout, Act I units, midpoint trigger,
persistence behavior, finale units, secured consequence, copy, character mood,
motion fallback, or accessibility behavior.

It does not claim that runtime implementation is complete or that every passage
candidate has passed source, rights, factual, grade-level, sensitivity, and
microphone review.

## Global story and final breach

The ten sites remain independently authored inside one count-based campaign
spine. The hub and Case File are specified in
[`CAMPAIGN_HUB_RUNTIME_BRIEF.md`](../gameplay/CAMPAIGN_HUB_RUNTIME_BRIEF.md).

After a genuine `10 OF 10 SITES SECURED` finish, the singular
`EVIDENCE_11.LIVE` breach uses exactly three saved containment checkpoints:
trace origin, preserve evidence, and revoke access. Its complete builder pack is
[`FINAL_BREACH_RUNTIME_BRIEF.md`](../gameplay/FINAL_BREACH_RUNTIME_BRIEF.md),
with original assets under
[`art/endgame/`](../../apps/internet-recovery/art/endgame/README.md).

## Concept-board boundary

Generated PNG boards remain useful for broad composition, story tone, and
midpoint/resolution comparison. They are not runtime backgrounds. Any embedded
`WIP`, `REVIEW ONLY`, `CONCEPT BOARD`, generated microcopy, real-product-like
mark, or legacy browser chrome is exploration history and is superseded by this
production index, the exact site contracts, the copy manifest, and the original
SVG marks.

## Builder consumption order

1. Keep the implemented Recovery Desktop and shipped WikiWhy reader/campaign
   baseline.
2. Preserve ThreadIt and FacePlace as deployed portability proofs while their
   provisional fixtures await canonical replacements.
3. Publish and verify the implemented local MapGuess vertical slice without
   promoting its provisional fixture or slot-10 test receipt.
4. Build MyCorner, Yahuh! Portal, ViewTube, Search-ish, Amaze-On, and Spotty-Fi
   one vertical slice at a time.
5. Keep unimplemented sites in truthful preview mode without borrowing another
   site's passage or rules.
6. After ten real canonical sites, build the separate final breach from its
   dedicated pack.
