# WikiWhy reading decks

This folder contains WikiWhy-specific reading content. Deck A is the initial
ten-passage pool; Deck B is the first replay pool. A future memory system may
record passage IDs already served and prefer an unseen deck on replay.

The passages in `PASSAGE_DECKS.md` are adapted from the linked English Wikipedia
articles. Wikipedia contributors are credited through each article URL and its
linked page history. The adaptations simplify, reorganize, and add transitions
for grades 10–12 read-aloud practice; they are not verbatim article excerpts.

The adapted passages are distributed under the
[Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International license](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/).
Changes from the source are indicated here and in every passage record. No
Wikipedia images, logos, or other non-text media are included.

Article content changes over time. Before production publication, preserve a
permanent revision link and review each passage for factual accuracy,
transcription behavior, reading level, and additional source notices.
