# Rogue-AI concept-board prompt notes

These review notes preserve the final prompt direction for the July 2026
rogue-AI visual-canon pass. The boards were created with Codex's built-in image
generation in reference-edit mode. Private character and dog references were
used during generation and remain outside the repository.

## Shared prompt block

Every revised board was asked to:

- retain the established crisp Internet Recovery 98 concept-board style;
- present the game as a remote tunnel into Finn's fictional desktop, with the
  site browser visibly inset and a stable desktop rim left around it;
- use that rim for restrained period-computer Easter eggs such as an original
  trash/recycle-bin parody, folders, files, shortcuts, compact taskbar, and an
  honest `Remote Tunnel Active` indicator;
- keep the continuous Reading Companion visually separate and trustworthy;
- depict Chinmay with shoulder-length black hair in every appearance, moving
  from neat confidence to increasingly messy, frazzled, flustered concern but
  never anger, menace, or villain coding;
- depict a nonhuman cyan/violet AI process as the agent making unauthorized
  writes;
- keep Techno close to her orange-and-blue ball whenever she appears; and
- use private photos for likeness only, never as pasted panels or photographic
  output; Amy, Chinmay, and Techno share the character board's clean inked 2D
  editorial-cartoon treatment, stable proportions, and warm cel shading; and
- use compact, natural webpage typography suitable for a 14-year-old, with the
  image explicitly framed as a review concept rather than production UI.

## Production character-sheet pass

The production sheets under `apps/internet-recovery/art/characters/` were
generated with Codex's built-in image-generation workflow from the reviewed
concept boards as style and identity references. The final prompts intentionally
removed embedded text labels; the asset IDs live in the character manifest so
the builder is not dependent on generated lettering.

Shared character-sheet constraints:

- clean inked 2D editorial-cartoon style with warm cel shading;
- 1536x1024 landscape sheets on a clean cream background;
- consistent framed panels, scale, line weight, and readable silhouettes;
- no photorealism, no pasted photos, no real logos, no watermark, and no WIP
  labels;
- private references remain likeness-only and are not committed.

The final sheets are:

- `../characters/amy-production-portraits.png` - six Amy support states:
  neutral, skeptical, amused, supportive, evidence, and tools.
- `../characters/chinmay-production-portraits.png` - six long-haired Chinmay
  states: neutral, confident, fluster 1, fluster 2, fluster 3, and relieved.
- `../characters/techno-production-sprites.png` - eight Techno states centered
  on her original orange-and-blue grooved ball.
